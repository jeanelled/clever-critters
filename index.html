Stories that make big ideas feel simple.

In early education, there is a gap: STEM books can feel too abstract for young children, while many storybooks skip meaningful technical ideas. The Clever Critters Collection is a series of illustrated storybooks that introduce foundational STEM concepts through rich, character-driven storytelling. With Sprout, Coral, and their friends, engage your curiosity and explore the world of STEM!

Created by Jeanelle Dao and Jadelynn Dao, the Clever Critters Collection blends character-driven adventures with clear, kid-friendly foundations in logic and statistics.

Books that bridge the gap between meaningful storytelling and early STEM education.

Garden Gates

An Introduction to Boolean Logic

Ages 7-11 STEM Learning Logic Basics

In this bright and buzzing garden, meet Sprout and clever critters who solve problems together, just like computers do. Garden Gates introduces kids to Boolean logic, the language of computer engineers and the foundation of every digital device. Perfect for curious minds and future tinkerers, the story is a fun start to early STEM learning.

Sea of Stats

An Introduction to Descriptive Statistics

Ages 7-11 Data Literacy Math Confidence

In this sparkling sea, meet Coral and her curious sea friends as they explore patterns, compare treasures, and make sense of their ocean world, just like statisticians do. Sea of Stats introduces kids to descriptive statistics, the way we collect, organize, and describe numbers all around us. Perfect for budding explorers and little data divers, this story makes quantitative problem-solving feel like an underwater adventure in discovery.
