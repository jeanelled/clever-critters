Partnership with Union School District

We're thrilled to partner with Union School District to bring the Clever Critters Collection to over 5,400 students across the 8 schools in the district. Together, we're committed to making STEM education accessible, engaging, and inclusive for all learners. Through this partnership, we've donated books and educational resources to the school libraries and worked on engaging activities for the district's numerous STEAM Nights! We look forward to growing this partnership and inspiring the next generation of STEM explorers!
