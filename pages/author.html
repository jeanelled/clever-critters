About The Author

Jeanelle Dao

Jeanelle Dao is the author of the Clever Critters Collection, a children's book series designed to make foundational STEM ideas understandable, joyful, and story-driven.

About Me

I've always been passionate about STEM education and storytelling. From Lego robotics to hosting engineering workshops, I believe in the power of creativity and curiosity to make learning fun and meaningful. With the Clever Critters Collection, I wanted to create a series of books that not only introduce foundational STEM concepts but also inspire young readers to see themselves as capable problem-solvers and adventurers in the world of STEM.

Participating in international programs such as the US State Department's TechGirls program, I have had the opportunity to connect with young people around the world and work together to inspire curiosity and creativity. These experiences have deeply influenced my approach to writing and education, reinforcing the importance of making STEM accessible and engaging for all children, regardless of their background or where they live.
